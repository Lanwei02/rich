About Me…

I’m the Assistant Vice President of Donor Relations and Advancement Strategy at Colby College, where I work to develop new approaches to modeling and visualizing fundraising data. I teach R workshops and am listed in RStudio’s directory of instructors. Additionally, I’m an instructor on DataCamp and a data science mentor with SpringBoard. Previously, I was a founder of Third Coast Analytics (TCA), a data science consulting firm (TCA’s IP was acquired by Capture Higher Ed in 2016).
